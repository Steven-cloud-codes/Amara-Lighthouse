About Amara Lighthouse

Amara Lighthouse is more than a project — it’s a digital beacon preserving Myanmar’s culture, history, and traditions. Our journey began with a simple idea in the Ancient Revival coding tournament, but quickly grew into a mission: to bring Myanmar’s untold stories, landmarks, and heritage to the modern world through an engaging, interactive platform.

As a team of passionate young creators, we believe history should be experienced, not just read. Our site blends rich storytelling, beautiful visuals, and a cultural e-commerce section where visitors can discover and purchase authentic items. In doing so, we also aim to support local artisans and small businesses, helping preserve traditional craftsmanship for future generations.

Amara Lighthouse stands as a bridge between past and present — a place where curiosity meets culture, and where exploring Myanmar feels as exciting as discovering a hidden treasure.
