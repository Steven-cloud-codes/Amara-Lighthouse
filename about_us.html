About Amara Lighthouse

Amara Lighthouse is more than a project — it's a revival of Myanmar's untold stories and timeless heritage. What started as a simple assignment turned into a journey through ancient scripts, forgotten kingdoms, and cultural treasures. We turned boredom in history class into curiosity, and curiosity into creation.

Our mission is to make learning history fun, visual, and meaningful for the younger generation. Through storytelling, games, and interactive design, we bring the past to life — because to shape the future, we must first understand our roots.
