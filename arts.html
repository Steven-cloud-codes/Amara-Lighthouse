Famous Myanmar Authors

Khin Khin Htoo

Born in 1965. A popular modern writer known for realistic and emotional novels like Ma Thudamasari. Her stories often center on women's lives, love, and rural Myanmar.

Ju

Contemporary writer known for modern themes and urban stories. His works often explore the challenges of youth in Yangon.

Phay Myint

Born in 1930, died in 2006. A respected Myanmar journalist, writer, and editor. Known for his sharp political articles and historical writings. He was the editor of several well-known journals and contributed to the development of independent journalism in Myanmar. Also known for biographies and essays on political leaders.
